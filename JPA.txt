JPA Lecture 1:(Jakarta Persistence API)

- JPA is nothing but specification.
- In JDBC queries are executed on the database.
- In JPA queries are executed on the context, the context contains objects(entities),
  now when operation are performed they are done on context not on DBMS.
- Now the context interacts with database accordingly


- Through EntityManager we operate on context, and we get it through EntityManagerFactory

  
EntityManagerFactory emf = Peristence.createManagerFactory()

EntityManager em = emf.createEntityManager()


em.getTransaction().begin()

Product p = new Product()

em.persist(p); // add this to context--> not an insert query


em.getTransaction.commit()
